    Serp.farm Reviews 

    This serp.farm review is based on my own experiences as are all reviews on this site. I've been using Serp.farm for over a year now and am very impressed. I started with mass page builders back in the days of Traffic Equalizer and have used others such as Source Flood and Serpshaker.

    Serpfarm is a Wordpress plugin that can create thousands of pages (or more) in no time. For one project I created about 800,000 pages and at one point about 250,000 were indexed. Here's a video that shows how Serp.farm works.
